## Types of product announcements entries Overall, release notes are pretty standard and straightforward. Depending on the nature of your new release, you can borrow certain parts from each release note entry (which is what we did with Canny’s Changelog). There are a few types of posts you’d add to your release notes. Let’s discuss each one. ### Major update announcement Intended audience: users, leads, team members, shareholders When you’re adding a major new feature, make sure to give this announcement the attention it deserves.
