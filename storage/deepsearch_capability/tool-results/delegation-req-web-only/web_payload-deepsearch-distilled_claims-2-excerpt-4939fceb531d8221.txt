## What release notes are and how they work Release notes are a focused record of changes in a specific product version. They summarize new features, fixes, enhancements, and any notable updates to a product or service. The most effective release notes explain who benefits, how to use the updates, and where to find more information. Some teams treat release notes as a strict changelog. Others treat them like mini product updates or content for a blog. Both work. What matters is clarity, consistent structure, and relevance for the audience.
